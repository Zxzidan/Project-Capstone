Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Project: Finova AI (ID: 9761210568567244669)

Version: 1.0.0 | Date: May 1, 2026

1. Overview

Finova AI is a next-generation personal finance application designed to help users track expenses, manage budgets, and gain intelligent insights into their spending habits through AI-powered analytics.

2. Target Audience

  • Young professionals looking to manage their personal wealth.
  • Freelancers needing automated categorization for tax purposes.
  • Anyone looking to optimize their savings and reduce unnecessary subscriptions.

3. Core Features

3.1 Intelligent Dashboard

A unified dashboard available on both Desktop and Mobile that provides a quick overview of Total Balance, Monthly Spending, and AI Savings Goal progress. It includes recent transaction lists and interactive cash flow charts.

3.2 Seamless Transaction Entry

Users can quickly add expenses or incomes using a streamlined interface. The mobile app features a quick "Numpad" style entry for on-the-go tracking.

3.3 AI-Powered Insights

The system analyzes spending velocity and category trends to provide actionable recommendations. For example: detecting an unused subscription, warning about high dining expenses, or projecting end-of-month balances.

4. Design & Aesthetics

The application must use a "Super-App" modern aesthetic:

  • Glassmorphism: Blurred backgrounds and semi-transparent panels.
  • Color Palette: Deep space background (#0f172a) with vibrant accents (Primary Blue, Secondary Purple, Success Green).
  • Typography: 'Inter' font for maximum readability and modern feel.

5. Future Roadmap

  • v1.1: Plaid integration for automatic bank syncing.
  • v2.0: Collaborative budgets for households and couples.
  • v2.5: AI Chatbot assistant for conversational finance queries.
